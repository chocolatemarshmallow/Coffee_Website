Student Study Night

Bring your student ID and enjoy 20% off drinks after 6 PM Monday–Thursday. Perfect for late-night cram sessions or group study dates. Our cozy café offers free Wi-Fi, charging stations, and a quiet space to focus — plus, a little caffeine boost to keep you going!

OPM Acoustic Nights

Enjoy live performances by local OPM bands every Friday night. Sip your favorite brew while discovering rising talents and singing along to familiar hits. It’s a night of coffee, community, and Original Pilipino Music that feels like home.

Barkada Bundle

Bring your friends and enjoy group discounts on selected drinks. The more, the merrier — and the bigger the savings! Whether you’re catching up, celebrating, or just hanging out, this bundle makes every barkada moment extra sulit.

E-Sports & Coffee Day

Cheer for your favorite teams while enjoying our special brews. Watch live matches on our café screens, join mini gaming tournaments, or just vibe with fellow gamers. With unlimited refills on selected drinks, you’ll have all the energy you need to root for victory!

Filipino Delicacies Pairing

Pair coffee with bibingka, puto bumbong, and more every holiday season. A heartwarming celebration of Filipino flavors, this seasonal treat brings tradition and coffee together. Perfect for families, balikbayans, and anyone craving a taste of home.
